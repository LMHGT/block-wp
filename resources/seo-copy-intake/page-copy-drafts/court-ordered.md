# Court-Ordered Services in Louisville, KY

LMHG helps with some Family Court orders. We read the order first. It must name a service we offer. Or it must let LMHG pick the kind of care. Clear words help us check fit before you start.

These services may include Co-Parenting, Family Reunification, or mandated therapy tied to CPS involvement. LMHG does not give legal advice. We also cannot promise that a court will accept a service.

## When This May Help

This page may help if a Family Court has told you to take part in a service. You may need help with:

- an order that names a service and due date;
- Co-Parenting after conflict or a family change;
- Family Reunification after a break in parent-child contact;
- therapy ordered due to CPS involvement;
- proof that you came or took part; or
- a progress letter, specific recommendations, or testimony.

LMHG does not provide DUI evaluations. We do not handle criminal or substance-use court rules on this page.

## What Support Can Look Like

Start by sending the order, due date, referral, and rules about proof. We will read each word. We may ask what the court wants and who must take part.

The proof we can give is based on the service. It may show that you came or took part. It may be a short progress letter or treatment recommendation. LMHG may also give specific recommendations or testimony. This is common in some Family Reunification cases, but not in all services.

## How These Family Court Services Differ

[Co-Parenting Services](/co-parenting/) help one or both parents work on choices for their child and clear talks. Co-Parenting can also start before an order is made.

[Family Reunification Services](/family-reunification/) use set steps after a break in contact. Family Reunification is not therapy or counseling. The provider does not take a side. They may give testimony or specific recommendations when the case calls for it.

Court-mandated therapy tied to CPS is therapy. It may focus on your own needs and goals. [Individual Counseling](/individual-counseling/) may fit. It must match both the order and LMHG's review.

## Getting Started

Use the [Reach Out form](https://intakeq.com/new/g91Z8x/bjxuno). Add your name and a short note that you have a Family Court order. The form does not ask for health details. Someone from LMHG will ask for the order and check fit. Please do this before the service starts.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does LMHG accept every Family Court order?

No. The order must name a service LMHG offers or let LMHG pick the kind of care. We must read the order first.

### Is Family Reunification a type of therapy?

No. Family Reunification is its own service. It is not therapy or counseling.

### Can LMHG send records to the court?

At times. LMHG may give proof that you came or took part. We may also give a progress letter, recommendation, or testimony. It depends on the service and the case.

### Does LMHG provide DUI evaluations?

No. LMHG does not provide evaluations tied to DUI convictions.
