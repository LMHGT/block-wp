Individual counseling gives you a private place to talk with one main therapist. You can name what is hard, set goals, and shape the work to fit you. At Louisville Mental Health Group, each plan is made for the person. The method and pace can change as your needs change.

## When This May Help

Individual therapy may help with concerns that include, but are not limited to:

- Anxiety or depression
- Trauma or grief
- Stress, anger, or feeling stuck
- Strain in a relationship
- Work or school stress
- A move, loss, or other life change

You do not need to know the cause or the right therapy term before you start. You can begin with what is going on now and what you want to change.

## What Support Can Look Like

Early sessions help you and your therapist learn what brings you in. You can talk about goals, privacy, pace, and what may be useful. Part of the work is choosing therapy methods that fit why you came.

Most clients have one main therapist they see on a regular basis. At times, a second therapist may help with a focused need. For example, you may keep your main therapist and also meet with an [EMDR therapist](/emdr-therapy/). Each therapist has a clear role.

The same rule can help keep boundaries clear when couples work is added. One therapist may meet with the couple, while a different therapist provides one-on-one support.

LMHG accepts Medicaid, commercial insurance, and private pay when they fit the service and plan. See [Costs and Insurance](/insurance/) for more details.

## Individual Counseling And Related Therapy

Individual counseling is the broad one-on-one service. [Adult Counseling](/adult-counseling/) speaks to adult needs, while [Teen Therapy](/adolescent-counseling/) is written for teens and their families. Your therapist can help decide whether a more focused page or service fits your needs.

If family or couple talks may help, that work can be added with clear roles. It does not take away the private focus of your own therapy.

Use the [Reach Out intake form](https://intakeq.com/new/g91Z8x/bjxuno) to share your contact details and a short note about the help you want. The form does not ask for clinical details. LMHG will follow up about fit, timing, payment, and next steps.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is individual counseling private?

Yes. It is private one-on-one therapy, with legal and safety limits. Your therapist will explain those limits.

### How soon can I start?

Clients can often be seen within 7 to 14 days. A wait may be longer if you need a therapist with a specific trait or skill.

### Can I work with more than one therapist?

Yes, when it helps. You may have one main therapist and another for a focused need. Their roles stay clear.

### Do I need to choose a therapy method first?

No. You and your therapist can choose methods based on your needs and why you reached out.
