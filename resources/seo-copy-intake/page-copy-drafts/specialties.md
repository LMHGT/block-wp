Specialized therapy in Louisville, KY, may help when you know what you want to work on. LMHG has focused help for trauma, family needs, child behavior, parents, and daily life. Each type of help links back to a broad service. You can start broad if that feels more clear.

Specialty counseling in Louisville, KY, does not mean you must pick a method on your own. These pages tell you what each option is. They also say who it may help and what it cannot do. A provider can help you think about fit.

## Support By Age Or Concern

[Teen Therapy](/adolescent-counseling/) is for teens and families. It may help with mood, worry, school stress, trauma, or fights at home. [Adult Counseling](/adult-counseling/) gives adults a place to work on stress, grief, a close tie, or a big life change.

[Anxiety and Depression Therapy](/anxiety-depression-therapy/) focuses on worry, fear, low mood, and daily strain. LMHG offers therapy and counseling. It does not manage medicine.

## Child And Parent Specialties

[Child Behavioral Therapy](/child-behavioral-intervention/) helps parents look at what a child does at home or school. [Play Therapy](/play-therapy/) uses play with a clear goal. It is most often used with kids ages 2 to 12.

[Parent-Child Attachment Therapy](/attachment-therapy/) works on trust between a child and parent. [Parenting Support](/parenting-support/) helps with limits, home tasks, big feelings, and family stress.

## Trauma And Focused Therapy

[EMDR Therapy](/emdr-therapy/) is one set way to work on trauma. It may fit when a past event still feels stuck or too strong. EMDR is not right for each person. Trust and coping skills may need to come first.

The broad [Trauma Therapy](/trauma-therapy/) page may be a better start if a past event still affects you but you do not know which method may fit.

## Family And Court-Related Specialties

[Co-Parenting Services](/co-parenting/) help parents make child-focused choices. The work may start with a parent or a court order. It is not mediation or a custody review.

[Family Reunification Services](/family-reunification/) use a slow, fair process after a break in parent-child contact or trust. [Conflict Resolution Counseling](/conflict-resolution-counseling/) is for repeat fights among family members who are not a couple.

## Practical Mental Health Support

[Case Management](/case-management/) helps with plans, basic needs, links to aid, and team work. [Community Support Services](/community-support/) help clients use therapy goals in daily life with a support worker.

These services are not therapy. They may work next to therapy when a client needs more hands-on help.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is a specialty page?

A specialty page explains a focused concern, service, or therapy method. It helps you learn more before you choose a next step.

### Do I need a diagnosis to ask about a specialty?

No. You can start with what you notice and what you want help with. A provider can learn more before making a plan.

### Can a specialty work with regular therapy?

Yes. A client may keep a main therapist and add a focused provider or support service when clear roles would help.

### What if none of the pages seem right?

Start with the [Services page](/services/) or send a short request. LMHG can help you sort through the options.
