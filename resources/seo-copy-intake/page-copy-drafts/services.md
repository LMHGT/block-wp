Counseling services in Louisville, KY, can help with stress, family change, trauma, and daily needs. LMHG offers therapy, counseling, and hands-on help. You do not need to know the right name for the help you need.

Our mental health services in Louisville, KY, are for kids, teens, adults, couples, and families. Some people need one therapist. Others may need a small team. Each team member has a clear role. The first talk helps us learn what may fit.

## Therapy And Counseling Services

[Individual Counseling](/individual-counseling/) gives one person a private place to talk. It may help with stress, grief, trauma, or a life change. [Child Therapy](/child-counseling/) helps parents learn what a child may need. [Family Therapy](/family-therapy/) helps with fights and hard talks at home.

[Couples Counseling](/couples-counseling/) is for two partners. It may help with trust, closeness, or hard talks. [Group Therapy](/group-therapy/) brings clients and a group leader together. The group can learn and use new skills. Topics and open spots may change.

## Focused And Court-Related Services

[Trauma Therapy](/trauma-therapy/) may help when a past event still affects life now. The plan may use talk, grounding skills, EMDR, or other tools.

[Court-Ordered Services](/court-ordered/) may cover some Family Court needs. LMHG reads the order first. We do not give legal advice. We cannot say what a court will accept.

## Support Beyond The Therapy Room

[Community-Based Mental Health Services](/community-based-services/) can help when daily needs get in the way. This group of services includes Case Management and Community Support. It may also include work with a school or local group.

Case Management helps with plans, links to aid, and team work. Community Support helps clients use therapy goals in daily life. In-home therapy is not the same. It is a therapy visit held at home when that place is a good fit.

## Which Service May Fit?

Start with the need that feels most clear. A parent may start with Child Therapy. A person with a hard memory may start with Trauma Therapy. A person who needs help with food, a home, school, or a large team may start with Community-Based Services.

It is fine to be unsure. A short request can share how to reach you and the kind of help you want. LMHG can then talk with you about fit, wait time, cost, and who may help.

## Getting Started

Use the [Reach Out form](https://intakeq.com/new/g91Z8x/bjxuno) or call with a simple question. You do not need to put private health facts on the form. Someone from LMHG will then talk with you about next steps.

LMHG takes Medicaid, many health plans, and private pay when they fit the service. We must check your plan and open spots before work starts. See [Costs and Insurance](/insurance/) to learn more.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to choose a service before I reach out?

No. You can say what is happening and who needs help. LMHG can talk with you about which service may fit.

### Are all services therapy?

No. LMHG offers therapy and counseling, but it also offers Case Management and Community Support. Each service has a different role.

### Can one person have more than one provider?

Yes, when it helps and each provider has a clear role. A main therapist may work with another therapist or support team member.

### How do I ask about cost?

Share your insurance plan or ask about private pay. LMHG will check the service, plan, and coverage before work starts.
