# SEO Decision Lab Research: Remaining Rank Math Pages

Date: 2026-07-16  
Authority: SEO Decision Lab using DataForSEO Labs and live Google organic SERPs  
Scope: 30 published routes that were not included in the July 10 services/specialties map. `/sample-page/` is a draft and is excluded.

## Method

- Reused the most recent route-specific SEO Decision Lab decisions where they already existed.
- Ran a 30-keyword DataForSEO Labs keyword-overview batch for current volume, difficulty, and intent evidence.
- Reviewed live Google SERPs for Medicaid, in-home therapy, Bullitt County, and school-based mental-health queries.
- Used exact route intent for brand, legal, county, contact, and other utility pages where DataForSEO reported no meaningful volume record.
- Avoided assigning broad service terms to utility pages or duplicating the canonical owner of an existing service/specialty query.

Current API cost for this research pass was `$0.05648`: two keyword-overview batches (`$0.02688`), one exploratory keyword-ideas call (`$0.02160`), and four live SERPs (`$0.00800`).
